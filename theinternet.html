Network infrastructure can be a mix of hardware devices, software applications, and network services, including: Hardware infrastructure typically includes routers, switches, hubs, repeaters, gateways, bridges, and modems. Software infrastructure includes monitoring and management tools and operating systems.


history of the Web

The development of the World Wide Web was begun in 1989 by Tim Berners-Lee and his colleagues at CERN, an international scientific organization based in Geneva, Switzerland. They created a protocol, HyperText Transfer Protocol (HTTP), which standardized communication between servers and clients.
